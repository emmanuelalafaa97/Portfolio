About Me

Emmanuel Alafaa is a data science and geospatial analyst with expertise in SQL, Python, GIS, and data visualization. He is keen on applying machine learning alongside advanced geospatial mapping techniques, leveraging tools like QGIS, ArcGIS, and GeoPandas to analyze environmental challenges, infrastructure, and resource management.

Passionate about open-source mapping, Emmanuel actively contributes to Humanitarian OpenStreetMap and seeks opportunities at the intersection of data science, GIS, and environmental analytics. He is always eager to collaborate, using data-driven insights to address real-world challenges.
